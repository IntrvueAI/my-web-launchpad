Why we built this

An interview isn't a test.
It's a conversation.

Most children preparing for the 11+ only ever rehearse on paper. But the interview itself is spoken — a real conversation with a real adult, under real pressure. Confidence there doesn't come from memorising more answers. It comes from repetition: sitting the conversation itself, again and again, in real time, until it stops feeling like a test.
