Getting started

What intrvue.ai is and how a session runs.

It's a practice platform for the 11+ and independent school interview. You have a spoken mock interview with Clara, our AI interviewer, and get clear written feedback straight after — the same way you'd rehearse with a real tutor, but available whenever you need it.

Pick an interview type (school interview, maths, logic puzzles, or current affairs & moral reasoning), then talk to Clara out loud just like a real interview. She asks questions, listens to your answers, and moves the conversation forward naturally.

Talking to Clara

How the spoken conversation actually works.

Hold the push-to-talk button (or hold the T key) while you speak, then release when you're done answering — just like a walkie-talkie. Clara will reply out loud and the conversation continues.

You can switch to 'Type answers' at any point during a session if you're not comfortable speaking, though we'd always recommend practising out loud where you can — the real interview is spoken, so that's what builds the confidence.

It's a real conversation. Clara responds to what you actually say rather than working through a fixed script, so no two interviews play out exactly the same way — just like sitting in front of a real interviewer.

Use 'Skip this question' in the interview controls. Nothing is held against you for skipping — it simply moves you on to the next part of the interview.

Scoring & practical stuff

Feedback, credits, and privacy.

After each session you get a written score alongside specific feedback on what went well and what to work on next time, based on the same things real school interviewers look for.

Each full mock interview uses credits from your account, shown at the top of the page. The Free Demo Interview doesn't use any, so it's the best way to get a feel for things first.

Yes. Your sessions are only used to generate your feedback and are never shared or made public.
